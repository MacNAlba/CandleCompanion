Title: Candle Companion
Description: An app that allows entry and storage of products and materials information.
It begins with ingredients being entered then as products are made, the quantities of
ingredients go down according to what is used in each product. There should be a third page
which deals with sales that draws from the product list and reduces the numbers before
outputting a pdf invoice for the customer
Objects:
    Products table:
        Product
        Scent
        Decoration
        Quantity
        Price per unit

    Materials table:
        Ingredient
        Quantity
        Cost

    Data entry:
        add items from fields
        remove items
